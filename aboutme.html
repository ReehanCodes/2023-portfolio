Hello, my name is Reehan Chowdhury, and I am a Grade 12 student living in Ontario, Canada.

As an aspiring engineer, I am passionate about using my skills to contribute to the betterment of society. One area that particularly fascinates me is the medical industry, and I dream of working on projects that can make a meaningful impact on people's lives. In particular, I am interested in building prosthetic limbs for patients suffering from osteoarthritis, a condition that affects millions of individuals worldwide.

I am determined to apply my knowledge and expertise in engineering to create innovative solutions that can improve the quality of life for those in need. With a strong drive to make a positive difference, I am excited to pursue my engineering career and work towards making a tangible impact in the medical field.
